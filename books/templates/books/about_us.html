About Us

OnlineBookReader is a site for reading books. Provide us with a link to a text file, and we will split it into easily digestable pages. We'll save your current books, and keep track of your reading position.

OnlineBookReader does not provide books, we simply present them. The best place to find free, well-formatted books in the public domain is Project Gutenberg, a site designed with the mission statement of making public domain books easily accessible - our site was designed with these books in mind.

OnlineBookReader is not designed for books outside of the public domain, and encourages all users to check they are complying with their country's copyright law at all times. OnlineBookReader does not filter hate speech or adult language from books, so choose books at your own discretion and risk.
