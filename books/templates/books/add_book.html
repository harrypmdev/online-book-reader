Add a new book

Enter a URL to a text file hosted online to add it as a book. We recommend Project Gutenberg as a good place to find free, public domain books. Here are their current top 100 books in English: Gutenberg Top 100. Simply:

  1. Click a book.
  2. Under 'choose how to read this book', click 'Plain Text UTF-8'.
  3. Copy the URL in your browser's address bar and paste it here.
Books can be accessed elsewhere but formatting may vary and OnlineBookReader may be less successful at tasks like automatic title and author generation.
